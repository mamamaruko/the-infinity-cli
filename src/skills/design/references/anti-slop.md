# Anti-Slop Heuristics

Use this as a fast quality filter before shipping any design output.

## Fast Fail Checks

- Could this be mistaken for a default AI template?
- Is the visual hierarchy obvious without reading every word?
- Does the accent color have a product-specific reason?
- Is there at least one memorable, intentional detail?

## Craft Checks

- Typography feels deliberate, not random
- Spacing rhythm is consistent
- CTA hierarchy is clear
- Trust and objection handling are explicit

## Rationale Check

For each major design decision, answer:

- Why this pattern?
- Why for this audience?
- Why now in this flow?

If any answer is weak, revisit the design direction before implementation.
